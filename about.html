Our Story

We believe that time is more than just a measurement - it’s an expression of style, a symbol of moments, and a companion on life’s journey. Our passion for watches drives us to curate a collection that resonates with watch enthusiasts worldwide.Founded by Thomas Peterson in 1884, company emerged from a love affair with horology. Inspired by craftsmanship, innovation, and timeless elegance, we embarked on a mission to bring exceptional watches to discerning wrists.

Our Commitment

Our commitment extends beyond exquisite timepieces. We’re dedicated to creating a watch community where craftsmanship, authenticity, and shared moments converge. Quality First: Every watch we offer undergoes rigorous quality checks. From movement precision to material durability, we ensure excellence. Sustainable Practices: We embrace eco-conscious choices. Our packaging is recyclable, and we support ethical sourcing. Personalized Service: When you choose pur watches, you become part of our family. Expect personalized recommendations and attentive care. Events and Workshops: Join us for watch enthusiasts’ gatherings, workshops, and talks. Let’s celebrate horology together.
